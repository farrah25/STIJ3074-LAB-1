ABOUT THE RESTAURANT

The Amirul Thai & Malay Restaurant was founded at Kuala Terengganu by Mr. Amirul Aiman Bin Md Nasir. He started together with his lovely wife Norsuziana Binti Sakri. This restaurant has been operating since 2019 which means 2 years already. This restaurant features a variety of daily menus with a focus on Malay and Thai cuisine. Among the Malay cuisine menu is fried rice, chicken rice, fries, keropok lekor, colek buah and many more. For the Thai menu, our restaurant have many menus which is mee celup Thai, somtam mangga, pulut mangga, nasi air berlauk and so on. The restaurant also features a variety of drinks including apple juice, orange juice, watermelon juice, milo ice, teh ice, hot drinks and many more. When you visit the restaurant, don't forget to check the menus promotions that our restaurant provide to you!

Opening hours: every Saturday until Tuesday and will be closed Wednesday and operate from 01:00PM until 12:00AM.

Address: Lot 854, Jalan Kuala Berang Kampung Merbau Patah (Amirul Thai & Malay Restaurant) Kuala Terengganu, 20050 Terengganu.
